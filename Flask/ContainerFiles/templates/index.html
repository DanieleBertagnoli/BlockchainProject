HOW DOES DragonBlock WORK?

CREATE NEW CAMPAIGNS

You can create your own campaign to gather funds for your projects! The amount of ETH that you can request is proportional to your participation in the platform. Be careful, you have to deposit 5% of what you are requesting, so make sure that your campaign aligns with your needs and is interesting for other users; otherwise, you could lose money!

DONATE TO EXISTING CAMPAIGNS

You can donate to other existing campaigns, supporting other creators! We also protect you from scams whenever possible; if a campaign is banned before it ends, we will refund your donation.

REPORT MALICIOUS CAMPAIGNS

Help us maintain the platform as a secure place. If you believe that a campaign is malicious, you can report it. If the ban is confirmed, you will also be rewarded with new DSTs!

EARN DragonSphereTokens

Earning DSTs is fundamental to becoming an important user on the platform. The more DSTs you have, the higher your importance in the platform. You can earn them either by donating (0.0005 ETH = 1 DST) or by reporting malicious campaigns, thereby helping us ban scammers!

WHAT A SUPER SAIYAN USER IS?

A Super Saiyan User (SSJ-User) on DragonBlock is a distinguished member of our crowdfunding community, wielding significant influence and responsibility. Just like the legendary Super Saiyans in the Dragon Ball universe, SSJ-Users play a pivotal role in the success and integrity of our platform.

WHY SHOULD I BECOME A SUPER SAIYAN USER?

Becoming a Super Saiyan User is a transformative journey that comes with exclusive benefits and opportunities.

Influence and Decision-Making

Gain the power to approve or deny campaigns, shaping the direction of the platform.

Rewards and Incentives

Enjoy financial rewards for your active participation and successful campaign evaluations.

Exclusive Exit Option

Retain the flexibility to exit the SSJ-User set and access the value accumulated in your vault.

Promoting Platform Security

Contribute to the security of the platform by voting on pending campaigns.

HOW CAN I BECOME A SUPER SAIYAN?

Become a SSJ is very simple, it is sufficient to have at least 500 DSTs.

WHY SHOULD I NOT BECOME A SUPER SAIYAN USER?

While becoming a Super Saiyan User is a transformative journey with exclusive benefits, there are factors to consider before taking on this role:

Responsibility and Decision-Making Pressure

Being an SSJ-User means having the power to approve or deny campaigns, which comes with significant responsibility and decision-making pressure.

Staking Requirement

To become an SSJ-User, users need to stake 0.5 ETH, which might be a financial commitment some users are not willing to make.

Time Commitment

Active participation as an SSJ-User requires timely voting on pending campaigns, which might be challenging for users with limited time.

Risk of Campaign Misjudgment

SSJ-Users play a crucial role in evaluating campaigns, and misjudging the legitimacy or potential success of a campaign could have repercussions.
